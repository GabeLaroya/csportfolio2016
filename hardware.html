Motherboard

A Motherboard is the part of the computer that connects various components of the computer together.. This serves as the control center of the computer. It lets the Central Proccesing Unit control other parts of the computer. Motherboards have modular capabilites, meaning they can have different ports aded to them to fit the needs of the user. THe first motherboard was created in 1981 by IBM.

CPU

The cpu is the part of the computer that executes commands of the user though the computer. The CPU or Central Procssing Unit, is the brain of the computer. The first CPU was created in the 1940s by J. Presper Eckert. It's first incarnation was used in a stored computer, a huge machine that store it's programs using electronic memory.
